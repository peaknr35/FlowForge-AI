# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 20, 2026**

FlowForge AI ("we", "us", "our") operates the flowforge-ai.com web application. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## 1. Data We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account details**: Email address and name, collected via Google OAuth during sign-up.
- **Generation history**: Inputs you submit and outputs produced by the five AI modules (Workflow Builder, Business Plan Generator, Cold Email Sequencer, Contract Analyzer, Niche Copy Engine).
- **Usage statistics**: Module usage counts, generation counts, and session duration for plan-limit enforcement and product improvement.

### Automatically Collected

- **Device data**: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution.
- **Log data**: IP address, request timestamps, HTTP status codes. Retained for 90 days for security and debugging.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

- Provide and operate the service.
- Enforce plan-level usage limits.
- Process subscription payments.
- Send transactional emails (receipts, password resets).
- Improve product performance and reliability.
- Respond to support requests.

We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

## 3. AI Processing -- Your Data Stays Local

FlowForge AI runs all AI inference through Ollama on your local machine. We do not send your inputs, generated outputs, or any document content to external AI APIs. Processing happens entirely within your environment.

## 4. Third-Party Services

### Supabase

We use Supabase for authentication and database storage. Supabase servers are located in the United States. Your account details, generation history, and usage data are stored with Supabase. See [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy).

### Stripe

We use Stripe to process payments. Card data is sent directly from your browser to Stripe and never passes through or is stored on our servers. See [Stripe Privacy Policy](https://stripe.com/privacy).

### Google OAuth

We use Google OAuth for sign-in. We receive your email address and name from Google. We do not request access to your email contents, contacts, or other Google data. See [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with any third parties except as described above (Supabase for storage, Stripe for payments, Google for authentication). We will disclose data if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

## 6. Data Retention

- Account data: Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
- Generation history: Retained while your account is active. Deleted with your account.
- Log data: 90 days.
- Billing records: Retained as required by law (typically 7 years).

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Export** your data. Request a copy of all data we hold about you.
- **Delete** your data. Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
- **Correct** your data. Request correction of inaccurate information.

To exercise any of these rights, contact peaknr35@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

## 8. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encrypted transit (TLS) and encrypted storage at rest via Supabase. No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. Children's Privacy

FlowForge AI is not intended for anyone under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If we learn we have collected such data, we will delete it promptly.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will notify you by email and by updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
